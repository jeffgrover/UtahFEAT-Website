Welcome to UtahFEAT.org!

Our Mission: To provide education, support and guidance to Utah families of children on the autism spectrum, and to enhance the community of professionals and educators serving those families.

Learn More Contact Us

More Details about UtahFEAT:

Utah Families for Effective Autism Treatment (F.E.A.T.) is a non-profit agency founded in 2006 by a group of parents with children diagnosed with autism and related disorders. Our vision is to create a resource for families in which vast amounts of information would be available in a personal way. Our parents and other volunteers provide encouragement, information and support beginning with those first days of an autism spectrum diagnosis and continuing across the lifespan of our children.
